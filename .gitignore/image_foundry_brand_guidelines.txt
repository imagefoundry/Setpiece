=======================================================
1. LOGO
=======================================================

LOGO MARQUE — SOLID
- Primary logo (the "F" shaped marque)
- Use standard or reversed as first version of logo to introduce the brand
- Works equally well in any of the three brand colours

LOGO MARQUE — REVERSED
- Logo reversed white out of each of the three brand colours:
  IF Pink, IF Blue, IF Yellow

LOGO MARQUE — MONO APPLICATION
- Only for specific mono applications: black and white animation/video
- Also for print limitations: photocopying, fax, black & white printers
- Use full colour logos in all other instances

LOGO MARQUE — WIREFRAME
- Used to support the solid marque
- Suitable as a regular logo or graphic device
- May appear in any of the three brand colours on a pale background

LOGO MARQUE — WIREFRAME REVERSE
- Wireframe marque reversed white out of any of the three brand colours

LOGO CLEAR SPACE
- Always maintain a 'safe area' of clear space around the logo
- Clear space = width of the vertical section of the marque (referred to as 'X')

LOGOTYPE — COLOUR
- May be used alone or with the solid logo marque for greater clarity

LOGOTYPE — REVERSE
- Reversed white out of any of the three brand colours

LOGOTYPE — MONO APPLICATION
- Only for black & white animation/video or limited print (photocopying, fax, B&W printers)
- Use full colour logos in all other instances

LOGOTYPE CLEAR SPACE
- Always maintain a 'safe area' equal to the height of the letters on all sides

WATERMARK
- The IF watermark pattern (repeating diamond/marque pattern) can be overlaid over imagery

COLOUR RATIO
- Use primary colour at 90%, secondary accent at no more than 10%
- Secondary colours should ONLY be used alongside their associated primary hue
  (e.g. Secondary Pink only with Primary Pink, not mixed across groups)

COLOUR COMBINATIONS
- Combinations should be restrained to ensure results are graphic, bold and legible
- Approved combinations: Pink on white, Blue on white, Yellow on white
- Text can be placed over solid colour backgrounds using reversed (white) type

=======================================================
3. TYPEFACE
=======================================================

PRIMARY TYPEFACE: BARLOW CONDENSED
Google Fonts: fonts.google.com/specimen/Barlow+Condensed

USE FOR HEADLINES & STATEMENTS:
- Always UPPERCASE (capitalised for maximum impact)
- Modern, grotesk-style typeface — bold, robust
- Available weights: Bold, Medium, Regular

HEADLINE TRACKING:
- Apply +100 letter-spacing/tracking to all headlines
- This complements the logotype and allows letters to breathe
- Example: "IMAGE FOUNDRY" with wide letter spacing

BODY TEXT:
- Barlow Condensed in sentence case (not all caps)
- Use Regular, Medium, or Bold weights only
- Do NOT use italic or other weights
