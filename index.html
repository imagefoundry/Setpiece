How it works

One setpiece.

You send us the assets

Photography, CGI, brand materials. If we made the CGI for you originally, we already have it on file.

02 / Story

A 20-minute call to lock the narrative arc, the music direction and the cuts you need. You sign it off. We get to work.

04
The story shape

Every setpiece tells a four-part story.

The best property films don't just show the development. They take the viewer somewhere. Setpiece is built around a four-act arc that flexes to every brief.

Act 01

The Place

The location. The neighbourhood. The lifestyle context. The viewer arrives in the world the development sits in.

Act 02

The Arrival

The development emerges from its surroundings. Wide shots, hero angles, the moment the building reveals itself.

Act 03

The Life

Interiors. Residents. Light, texture and atmosphere. What it actually feels like to live there.

Act 04

The Proposition

The closing line. The address. The reason to find out more.

Story comes first. Motion follows.
That's what separates Setpiece from an AI video tool.
