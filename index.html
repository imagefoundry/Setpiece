01
The shift

Video has become the
centrepiece asset.

People consume video differently. They stop for it. They watch it for longer. They understand more in less time.

In a showroom, video often becomes the asset people gather around. Online, it is built for the channels buyers already use — Instagram, YouTube, Facebook, LinkedIn, portals, paid campaigns and email.

A good property film says a lot before anyone reads a line of copy. The camera moves, sequencing, music, editing and finish all suggest care, confidence and production value. That value reflects back on the development itself.

The problem is that traditional video production is expensive, slow and difficult to organise. Crews, weather, access, permissions, editing, locations and production management all add cost and complexity.

Setpiece removes that barrier. We take the CGI, photography and brand assets you already have and turn them into polished, cinematic video content without a shoot, without a large production budget, and without weeks of planning.

04
The story shape

Production value is not just resolution.

A film feels expensive because of how it is directed.

  • The camera movement.
  • The sequence of shots.
  • The rhythm of the edit.
  • The music.
  • The restraint.
  • The way the story builds.

Setpiece uses those film-making principles to make existing assets feel more premium, more intentional and more complete. Every Setpiece follows a simple property story arc.

Act 01

The Place

The location, neighbourhood and wider setting. The viewer understands where the development sits and why it matters.

Act 02

The Arrival

The development reveals itself. Exterior angles, approach shots and hero moments create presence and scale.

Act 03

The Life

Interiors, amenities, light, texture and atmosphere. This is where the scheme begins to feel desirable and lived-in.

Act 04

The Proposition

The final message: the address, offer, availability, launch line or reason to enquire.
